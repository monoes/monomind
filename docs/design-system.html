Image Style

Warm light on dark surfaces. Gold on espresso. The monoes visual world is tactile — real materials, soft focus, quiet compositions.

Tactical
Brass instruments on aged wood. Warm light, single subject, quiet precision.
Organic
Gold suspended in warm light. Soft bokeh, dark ground, ambient warmth.

Prompt Guide

Formula [gold/amber subject] on [dark espresso surface], warm light, [tactical or organic] feel
Examples soft golden light on dark wood table, single brass compass, warm macro photo

gold leaf flakes floating in warm amber liquid, dark background, soft bokeh

aged leather journal on espresso wood desk, warm afternoon light, shallow focus

Palette

Stay in the espresso-to-gold range. No cool tones, no pure black or white. Signal colors belong in UI, not imagery.

Guidance

Do Warm directional lighting, real materials (wood, leather, brass, paper), shallow depth of field
Do Macro framing — show grain and texture. Mix geometric precision with organic warmth
Avoid Cool tones (blue, chrome, neon), flat illustration, people or faces, busy compositions
Avoid Stock photo aesthetics — staged setups, perfect symmetry, over-saturated colors

Overlay Rules

Do Darken images with filter: brightness(0.5–0.7) before placing text
Do Add a gradient or solid scrim — bottom-up for editorial, full for centered layouts
Do Use ivory (#FAF7F0) for headings, gold (#C8A97E) for kickers, 60% ivory for body
Avoid Text directly on unfiltered imagery — always needs a darkening layer for readability
/* Bottom scrim gradient */ background: linear-gradient(to top, rgba(26,18,8,0.85) 0%, transparent 100%); /* Full scrim */ background: rgba(26,18,8,0.5); /* Image darkening */ filter: brightness(0.6); /* Text hierarchy on dark images */ Kicker: #C8A97E, 10px mono, 0.2em tracking, uppercase Heading: #FAF7F0, clamp(20px, 3vw, 32px), weight 300 Body: rgba(250,247,240,0.6), 13px, max-width 40ch

Technical Graphics

Architecture diagrams and flow charts on dark espresso ground. Gold for primary flows, signal colors for system components, mono labels.

Architecture Diagram

Diagram Rules

Do Dark espresso background (#2A2318). Boxes use 10% tinted fills with 1.5px colored strokes
Do Labels in mono font, 8–9px. Primary nodes use signal colors, secondary use muted gold at 30% opacity
Do Connections: 1.5px solid for primary flows, 1px dashed for secondary relationships
Avoid White or light backgrounds for diagrams — always use espresso. No thick borders or drop shadows on boxes
